This is the Korean template for speed-quiz decks.

Replace title, category, difficulty, and words with your own content.

- **title**: Deck name in Korean (e.g., "동물 A팀", "영화 B팀")
- **category**: One of: animals, food, sports, movies, kpop, countries, jobs, brands, proverbs, historical-figures
- **difficulty**: One of: easy, normal, hard
- **words**: Array of objects with `term` (required, 1–10 chars typical) and optional `hint` (≤30 chars)

Rules:
- Minimum 10 words per deck
- All terms must be unique within the deck
- A and B decks of the same category should use distinct words for fair head-to-head play
- Hints are optional but recommended for normal/hard decks (they help beginner players)
- Easy decks typically have no hints
- Keep terms concise (names, places, common objects)
