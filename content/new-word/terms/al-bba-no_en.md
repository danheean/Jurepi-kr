---
term: Not My Problem
definition: |
  A Korean slang from "그건 내가 알 바 아니다" (that's not mine to care about),
  ending with the Gyeongsang-dialect question particle "-no." Used to cynically
  express total indifference to someone else's situation or problem.
examples:
  - "That's your problem, not mine—al-bba-no."
  - "Whether the company makes you work overtime, why would I care? Not my problem."
reading: /al-p͈a-no/
aliases: [why should I care, not my business]
origin: Spread from a League of Legends streamer broadcast in 2022, then across online communities.
---

Captures the detached, self-focused mood of today's online culture.
Often paired with "누칼협" (who held a knife to your throat?), a similar phrase.
