---
term: Dinner Menu Rec
definition: |
  Short for "저녁 메뉴 추천" (dinner menu recommendation).
  Used to ask for or share suggestions when you can't decide what to eat for dinner.
examples:
  - "Need a dinner menu rec, everyone—torn between chicken and tteokbokki."
  - "Taking dinner menu recs~ something I can cook in one bowl as a solo diner."
reading: /jʌ-me-tɕʰu/
aliases: [dinner rec, what to eat for dinner]
origin: Spread through online communities and SNS amid the trend of abbreviating everything.
---

Pairs with "점메추" (lunch menu rec) and shows up constantly in everyday chats
about what to eat, meal after meal.
